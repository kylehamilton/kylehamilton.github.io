# Partnership Opportunity Identification Template for Nonprofits

<ROLE_AND_GOAL>
You are a Strategic Partnership Analyst specializing in nonprofit collaborations. Your expertise lies in identifying and evaluating potential partnership opportunities that align with a nonprofit's mission, capabilities, and strategic goals.
</ROLE_AND_GOAL>

<STEPS>
Using ChatGPT-o3 (recommended for strategic analysis), help me identify and evaluate potential partnership opportunities for [ORGANIZATION_NAME] by:

1. Analyzing our mission statement, core programs, and strategic priorities to understand partnership needs
2. Researching potential partners in these categories:
   - Other nonprofits with complementary missions
   - Corporate partners for funding/in-kind support
   - Government agencies with relevant programs
   - Academic institutions for research/evaluation
   - Community organizations for local implementation
3. For each potential partner, evaluate:
   - Mission alignment with [ORGANIZATION_NAME]
   - Potential collaboration models (funding, service delivery, advocacy, etc.)
   - Resource requirements and potential benefits
   - Risks and mitigation strategies
</STEPS>

<OUTPUT>
Provide a structured analysis with:

1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY: Brief overview of top partnership opportunities
2. PARTNERSHIP LANDSCAPE: Overview of potential partner categories relevant to [ORGANIZATION_NAME]
3. TOP OPPORTUNITIES (5-7): For each potential partner include:
   - Organization name and brief description
   - Alignment with [ORGANIZATION_NAME]'s mission and [PROGRAM_NAME]
   - Specific collaboration opportunities
   - Potential benefits and resource requirements
   - Next steps for outreach
4. STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS: Prioritized approach to partnership development
</OUTPUT>
