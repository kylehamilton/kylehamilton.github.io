Nonprofit AI Cookbook for OpenAI Jam Day

Created by Decoded Futures

Your guide to getting started with prompting ChatGPT for common nonprofit challenges

What is this?

The AI Prompt Cookbook is your nonprofit's guide to getting started with artificial intelligence tools like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini. Instead of staring at a blank chat box wondering what to ask, this cookbook provides you with ready-to-use prompts that solve real nonprofit challenges.

How it's organized

We've categorized nonprofit AI solutions into 5 core problem types:

  • Create and Communicate - Content creation, communications, and storytelling
  • Automate the Admin - Repetitive task automation and administrative efficiency
  • Answer and Assist - Question answering, support, and guidance systems
  • Sort and Scan - Information categorization, evaluation, and prioritization
  • Learn and Decide - Data analysis, research, and strategic decision-making

Each problem has three complexity levels:

  • Low Complexity: Simple, copy-paste ready prompts that work great in ChatGPT's web interface. Perfect for beginners who want to get started quickly.
  • Medium Complexity: More structured templates with specific sections and recommendations for which AI model to use. Good for when you want more control over the output.
  • High Complexity: Comprehensive templates with detailed instructions, examples, and constraints. Best for complex tasks that need precise, professional results.

For Jam Day: Keep it simple!

Don't get overwhelmed by all the options. Focus on finding 1-2 templates that match your current challenges. You can always come back and explore more later.

Step-by-step guide (for first-time AI users):

  1. Browse the categories - Click through the 5 problem types below to see what kinds of challenges AI can help with
  2. Find your problem - Look for templates that describe challenges you're actually facing at your organization
  3. Start with Low complexity - These are designed to work immediately without any setup
  4. Copy the template - Click the "Copy Template" button on any template page
  5. Open ChatGPT - Go to chat.openai.com and create a free account if you don't have one
  6. Paste and customize - Paste the template into ChatGPT and replace the [PLACEHOLDERS] with your specific information
  7. Hit enter and iterate - Send your prompt, then ask follow-up questions to refine the results

Tips for success:

  • Be specific: Replace [ORGANIZATION_NAME] with your actual organization name, [MISSION_FOCUS] with your specific cause, etc.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for changes: If the first result isn't quite right, ask ChatGPT to "make it more formal" or "add more details about X"
  • Try multiple approaches: If one template doesn't work perfectly, try a different one from the same category
  • Save what works: Keep copies of prompts that work well for your organization

Remember: AI is a tool to help you work faster and more effectively, not to replace your expertise. You know your organization best - use these templates as starting points and customize them to fit your unique needs and voice.
