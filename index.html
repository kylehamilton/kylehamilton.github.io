About William Kyle Hamilton

Kyle earned his Bachelor of Arts degree in Psychology with a minor in Political Science from the University of California, Merced in 2012 and is now a volunteer research assistant at the Health Communications and Interventions Lab. Kyle serves as a board member for the UC Merced Alumni Association, as well as a board member for the Merced County Advisory Board on Alcohol and Drug Problems. Kyle currently works at the University of California, Merced as a Lab Assistant III in the School of Natural Science.

R Workshops

Mechanical Turk and AWS Workshop - December 5th, 2013 at UC Merced

This workshop was geared towards social science researchers who had never used Amazon Web Services or Mechanical Turk. Topics that were touched on included What is MTurk?, How much should you pay?, Installing MTurkR in RStudio, Accessing your AWS Security Credentials, Messaging Workers thought MTurkR, Sending SMS and Email messages though AWS, and Assigning Qualifications in MTurk.

**These slides were designed to be used as a reference. **
