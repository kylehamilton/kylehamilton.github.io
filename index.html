Biography

Kyle is a Ph.D. student at the University of California, Merced in the Psychological Sciences graduate program. He is advised by Dr. Linda Cameron and is part of the health psychology group. Kyle received his Associate of Arts in Social Science from Reedley College and his Bachelor of Arts in Psychology with a minor in Political Science from the University of California, Merced. Kyle creates statistical software utilizing both R and the R package Shiny. Currently, he serves as a member of the University of California, Merced Alumni Association board of directors.
