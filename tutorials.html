Tutorials and Workshops

Mechanical Turk and AWS Workshop – December 5th, 2013 at UC Merced
This workshop was geared towards social science researchers who had never used Amazon Web Services or Mechanical Turk. Topics that were touched on included What is MTurk?, How much should you pay?, Installing MTurkR in RStudio, Accessing your AWS Security Credentials, Messaging Workers thought MTurkR, Sending SMS and Email messages though AWS, and Assigning Qualifications in MTurk. These slides were designed to be used as a reference.
